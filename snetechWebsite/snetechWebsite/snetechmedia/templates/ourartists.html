S'22KILE

S'thuthukile Babalwa Langa known by her stage name "S'22KILE" born August 17, 2000 is a South African Mussician ,Performer and songwriter. Born and raised in the township KwaNoshezi Pietermaritzburg KwaZulu-Natal. She released her first single featuring Malungelo called Uvalo with Snetech Media , it received a lot of positive reviews from radio and social media platforms. Click here to listen to "Uvalo" S'22kile ft Malungelo - Uvalo

RAY T

Thato Rachel also known by her stage name Ray T, Born and Raised in Tembisa, she began her music career 2015. She released her first single with Snetech media titled "Ndabezitha" featuring Malungelo. Her latest single features Sdala B and Malungelo called "Ngiyazifela" which has received positive feedback from the listeners. Listen to her latest single: Ray T ft Sdala B & Malungelo - Ngiyazifela

SNOWLESA

Sinokhle Palesa Mswane, also known as Snowlesa, Born and Raised in Ladysmith, KZN. She signed with Snetech media in 2023 and released her first single with the label called ungangithini?. The song has got air plays from radio stations such as Ukhozi FM and people are showing love for the song. Listen to Snowlesa's single: Snowlesa - Ungangithini?

TSIMO

Tsimo, aka Tsimoloho Pearl Lekaota, a singer-songwriter coming straight out of the heart of the Free State. She was was scouted by Sntech media and she has since released a single with the label called Ngifuna Lo. The song was featured on the top 10 Afro Pop chart on Gagasi FM. Listen to the song on this link: Tsimo - Ngifuna Lo

BRUNO MASEMZA

Paul Brian, also known as Bruno Masemza is the producer for Snetech Media, he has produced songs such as Uvalo, Ngawe, Ngothando and many more along side Malungelo. He also has his own single called Ivusa abalele with Themba N Musiq: Listen to the song here: Bruno Masemza, Themba N Musiq - Ivusa Abalele
