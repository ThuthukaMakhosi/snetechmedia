Welcome to Snetech Media

Snetech media is a South African record label founded by Sinethemba Malungelo also known as Malungelo in the music industry. The aim of the record label is to uplift local artists and distribute their music to a big audience. To explore more about SNETECH MEDIA, keep scrolling or the menu options at the top.

FOUNDER

Malungelo who is a singer, song-writer, producer and business man founded SNETECH media in 2022 and has so far signed artists such as Ray T, S'thuthukile Langa a.k.a S'22KILE, Tsimo and other great artists. Malungelo has worked with the great artists such as Mduduzi Ncube, Nolwazi, Dj Cleo, Xowla and many more. He owns various businesses and also has a soccer team called the Malungelo team.

Malungelo has released two albums so far, namely Cirle Groove Vol 1 and Circle Groove Vol 2. To get his latest album, click on this link: Cirle Groove Vol 2.

Follow Malungelo on Instagram to get the latest updates.

ARTISTS

SNETECH Media has signed various artists which include Ray T, S'22KILE, Tsimo, Mini twinz, Snowlesa and our producer Bruno Masemza. All these artists have released singles which received positive feedback from the fans as well as various radio stations like Metro FM and Ukhozi FM. To find out more about our artists, follow this link: Our Artists.
